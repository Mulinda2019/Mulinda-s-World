Mulinda Israel Negondeni

Mulinda Negondeni was born on May 14, 2002, in Ha-Mabila village, to the late Mr Thizwilondi Israel Negondeni and Mrs Gloria Takalani Negondeni. He is the youngest of four children.

Early Life and Education

Mulinda began his educational journey at Mabila Primary School and later attended Matavhela Technical High School for secondary education.

From 2020 to 2023, he pursued a degree in Human Resource Management at the University of Venda (Univen).

Personal Life

Mulinda is a devoted born-again Christian. He is blessed with a beautiful daughter, Avhonaho Precious Negondeni.

Sports and Music

Mulinda has a passion for sports, particularly football (soccer), where he has won numerous individual and collective medals and trophies. Additionally, he has participated in athletics, earning three marathon medals.

Music plays a significant role in his life. He started playing musical instruments at the age of 7 and specializes in piano, drums, and bass guitar.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

Mulinda founded Israel Izzy Maintenance, a house painting and yard cleaning business.

Technical Pursuits

Currently, Mulinda is exploring the field of Computer Science, with expertise in:

  • Web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Web applications (Python Teacher, Pyguider)
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • App Development
  • Machine Learning

Future Aspirations

Mulinda aims to continue making significant contributions to the tech industry, inspiring others through his work, and fostering a community of innovators.
